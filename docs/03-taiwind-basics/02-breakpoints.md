# Breakpoints

What are the screen size breakpoints used by tailwindcss?

1. `sm` small: applies to screens that are 640px wide or larger
1. `md` medium: applies to screens that are 768px wide or larger
1. `lg` large: applies to screens that are 1024px wide or larger
1. `xl` extra-large: applies to screens that are 1280px wide or larger
1. `2xl` 2x extra-large: applies to screens that are 1536px wide or larger
